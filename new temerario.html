LAMBORGHINI TEMERARIO

WHERE VISIONARY DESIGN MEETS UNPARALLELED PERFORMANCE

1. Lamborghini Temerario GT

Overview: The GT variant of the Temerario is a masterpiece of luxury and speed, designed for discerning drivers.

  • Engine: 5.2L V10, producing 650 horsepower
  • Performance: 0-100 km/h in 3.2 seconds, Top speed: 330 km/h
  • Special Features: Adaptive Aerodynamics, Advanced Infotainment System, Alcantara and Carbon-Fiber Interior

2. Lamborghini Temerario Spyder

Overview: The Spyder is a convertible variant offering an open-top experience that amplifies the thrill of driving.

  • Engine: 5.2L V10, producing 620 horsepower
  • Performance: 0-100 km/h in 3.5 seconds, Top speed: 325 km/h
  • Special Features: Electrically Operated Roof, Premium Audio System, Aerodynamic Design

3. Lamborghini Temerario SV

Overview: The Super Veloce (SV) variant is engineered for track enthusiasts with aggressive styling and unmatched performance.

  • Engine: 6.5L V12, producing 750 horsepower
  • Performance: 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds, Top speed: 350 km/h
  • Special Features: Fixed Rear Wing, Lightweight Carbon Construction, Track-Tuned Suspension
