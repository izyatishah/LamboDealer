More About Our Offers

Discover more details about our exclusive purchase offers, service packages, and loyalty rewards designed to enhance your Lamborghini ownership experience.

1) Exclusive Purchase Offers

  • Limited-Time Discounts: Offer special price reductions for a limited period on select models, particularly on stock vehicles or outgoing models.
  • Loyalty Discounts: Offer exclusive discounts for repeat customers or those who have previously purchased a Lamborghini.
  • Trade-In Bonuses: Provide higher trade-in values for customers who want to exchange their current vehicle for a new Lamborghini, making it easier for them to upgrade.
  • Referral Program: Reward existing customers who refer new buyers with discounts, free services, or exclusive merchandise.
  • Bundle Offers: Offer packages that combine the purchase of a Lamborghini with other services such as extended warranties, maintenance packages, or accessories.

Book: Exclusive Purchase Offers

2) Service and Maintanence Offers

  • Complimentary Maintenance Package: Offer a free 1-year service package or a free first-year maintenance to new Lamborghini owners. This can include oil changes, tire rotations, and basic checks.
  • Discounted Service Packages: Provide discounted or bundled service packages that cover key maintenance services for the first few years of ownership.
  • VIP Service: Offer priority service appointments or access to a VIP concierge service for a seamless ownership experience.
  • Extended Warranty Offer: Provide a discount on extended warranties or a complimentary extended warranty for new buyers.

Book: Service & Maintenance Offers

3) Owner Loyalty & Exclusive Club Offers

  • Lamborghini Owner Club Membership: Offer new buyers an exclusive membership to a Lamborghini owners’ club, with access to private events, driving experiences, and networking opportunities.
  • VIP Treatment for Owners: Provide VIP services, such as priority service, access to exclusive Lamborghini events, or invitations to private car launches or exhibitions.
  • Anniversary Gifts: Celebrate milestones with your customers, such as the anniversary of their Lamborghini purchase, by offering complimentary services or exclusive gifts.
