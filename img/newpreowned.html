SELEZIONE LAMBORGHINI

CERTIFIED PRE-OWNED PROGRAM

OVERVIEW

Selezione Lamborghini Certified Pre-Owned is the official program that guarantees total peace of mind and safety for new buyers of pre-owned Lamborghini cars. The pre-owned Lamborghini is selected based on a thorough certification process and prepared for sale by qualified technicians of the House of the Raging Bull, using exclusively original spare parts. Customers can also take advantage of the Lamborghini warranty and roadside assistance service for up to 24 months. The Selezione Lamborghini Certified Pre-Owned program is only available at the Authorized Dealerships and Service Centers.

MODELS

THE SELEZIONE LAMBORGHINI CERTIFIED PRE-OWNED WARRANTY

.

Lamborghini pre-owned cars in the Selezione program are covered by an additional Lamborghini warranty ranging from a minimum of 12 to a maximum of 24 months (It may vary by country). The warranty covers parts and labor and ensures that all operations are carried out by qualified Lamborghini technicians using only original components. ALWAYS SAFE WITH A LAMBORGHINI PRE‑OWNED CAR: ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE To ensure your peace of mind, every car in the Selezione Certified Pre‑Owned program can rely on roadside assistance for the duration of the warranty. The service is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.* * Roadside assistance can be provided by partner companies in some countries.

LAMBORGHINI PRE‑OWNED: THE MAXIMUM QUALITY AND SAFETY

EXTERIOR: In order to be accepted into the program, Lamborghini pre-owned cars must have: covered less than 70,000 km/43,500 miles (100,000 km/62,000 miles for Urus) been used for less than 120 months undergone the routine maintenance operations prescribed by Automobili Lamborghini passed a thorough inspection test according to a specific check-list To be included in the Selezione Lamborghini Certified Pre‑Owned program, each car must pass more than 150 checks. All the main characteristics are checked, including for example: car history interior and exterior of the car, including the luggage compartment fluid levels mechanics and electronics tires, wheels and brakes Each car then undergoes diagnostic tests and a road test.

PRESTIGIOUS POWER. VERIFIED PERFECTION

This is perfection without limits. The vehicles hand-selected to enter the Selezione Lamborghini Certified Pre-Owned Program have been deemed excellent enough for this most prestigious certification. Every Selezione Lamborghini model maintains the integrity set forth by Sant’Agata Bolognese and cultivates the original purity of the vehicles worthy enough to enter the program. Pre-owned perfection can only be obtained at Authorized Lamborghini Dealers.
